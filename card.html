Siberian Husky

The Siberian Huskys are known as escape artists and were members of the search and rescue team in WWII.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Lifespan: 12~14 years
  • Average Size: Height:21~24 inches
  • Weight:45~60 lbs
  • Behavior: Howls rather than barks

The Siberian Husky is a breed directly descended from original sled dog originated in Northeast Asia.They were bred by Chukchi people who heavily relied on them during their daily hunting and family life. Therefore, after properly trained, the Husky can be great home pet and trusted with children.
